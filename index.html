Engagement Models

Two delivery models under consideration.

The work can be delivered externally by a vendor, or internally by the in-house Digital and Technology team. Both models are presented below on equivalent dimensions for direct comparison.

Model A · External Vendor Delivery
Work outsourced to one of the four vendors who submitted proposals.
AED26K to 175K
Range across the four Design + Development proposals received
  • Cash cost: AED 26K to AED 175K depending on vendor selected. Excludes ongoing retainers and platform subscriptions.
  • IP and source code transfer occurs at project completion per contract. Operational knowledge resides with the vendor.
  • Post-launch change requests billed at vendor hourly rate, typically AED 200 to 500 per hour.
  • Three of four vendors are based outside the UAE. One vendor (StarBerry) requires a 24-month support contract.
  • Delivery timeline: 10 to 24 weeks depending on vendor and scope.
Model B · Internal Team Delivery
Work delivered by the existing in-house Digital and Technology team.
AED22K
Direct cash cost: tooling, cloud hosting, contingency. Excludes loaded engineering effort.
  • Cash cost: AED 22K covering tools, cloud, contingency. Loaded engineering cost (salary plus overhead) is approximately AED 80K to 100K of effort.
  • Source code, design system, and CMS schema retained internally throughout the project.
  • Post-launch changes handled by the same team at no incremental cash cost. Capacity is shared with business-as-usual workload.
  • Delivery timeline: approximately 14 weeks. Subject to internal capacity and prioritisation against other commitments.
  • Risk concentration: key-person dependency on lead developer. Discovery and wireframe phases already complete (see Status section).
